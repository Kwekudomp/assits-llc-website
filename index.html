Step 1: Vm3 Purifier Pads & Powders attract and hold moisture, eliminating volatiles, moisture, malodor and microbes.

Step 2: Vm3 Purifier releases a proprietary Odor Neutralising Complex (ONC) which kills mold using natural essential oils.

Step 3: The ONC traps and removes malodor, microbes, volatiles and moisture on contact in the air and on surfaces.
